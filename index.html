Specialization in Shortcuts

A hallmark of Mr. Shri Iyyappan’s teaching is his ability to impart time-saving shortcuts that simplify the process of solving complex aptitude questions. His approach emphasizes:

  • Time-Saving Techniques:

    • He introduces a range of techniques designed to solve problems efficiently, helping students reduce the time spent on each question and thus address more problems within the exam’s time constraints.


  • Pattern Recognition:

    • Students are trained to identify common patterns and trends in aptitude questions, allowing them to apply specific shortcuts and solutions effectively. This skill is crucial for selecting the most efficient approach to a problem.

  • Mental Math Strategies:

    • Mr. Iyyappan teaches mental math techniques that enable students to perform calculations quickly and accurately without heavy reliance on paper or calculators. These strategies are vital for improving speed and precision in aptitude tests.

  • Simplified Formulas:

    • Complex formulas are broken down into simpler, easy-to-remember shortcuts. This simplification aids students in recalling and applying these methods efficiently during the exam.

Effective Approaches to Aptitude Questions

In addition to shortcuts, Mr. Shri Iyyappan emphasizes strategic approaches that enhance problem-solving efficiency:


  • Understanding Question Types:

    • He ensures that students are adept at recognizing and tackling various question types, including quantitative, logical reasoning, and data interpretation. Knowing the nature of each question allows students to apply the most appropriate shortcuts and strategies.

  • Practice and Application:

    • Extensive practice with diverse aptitude questions is encouraged to reinforce shortcut techniques and strategies. Regular practice helps students internalize these methods and apply them effectively under exam conditions.

  • Analyzing Mistakes:

    • An integral part of Mr. Iyyappan’s teaching involves analyzing mistakes to understand where shortcuts can be refined or applied differently. This reflective approach helps students improve their techniques and avoid common pitfalls.

  • Building Confidence:

    • Mastery of shortcuts and strategies boosts students' confidence, equipping them to approach the exam with a positive mindset and readiness.
